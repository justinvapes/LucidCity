Lucid Driving School

Welcome to Driving School

All citizens of Lucid must pass drivers test and theory test before they can drive.

Theory Test
- The Theory Test costs $200, this is not refunded if you fail the test.

Driving Test
- The Driving Test costs $500, this payment will not be refunded if you fail as well.

Driving License
- Driving license document can be bought in Drivering school's office for 50$

Driving licence categories
A - You can drive any motorcycles
B - You can drive single vehicles that isn’t hitched to a trailer (Basic license for driving personal vehicles)
C - You can drive large, heavy vehicles. (Such as large trucks)
